## Preparation
- 2 MANGOES CHOPPED
- BLEND MANGOES, SUGAR 100G, MILK 1 CUP
- IN ANOTHER BOWL, CORNFLOUR 4 TB SPOON, HALF CUP MILK, MIX, SET ASIDE
- ADD THE MANGO BLEND TO PAN, STIR CONTINUOUSLY.
- ADD CORNFLOUR MIX WHILE STIRRING MANGO BLEND.
- MEDIUM LOW FLAME FOR 2 MINS UNTIL BIG BUBBLES FORM.
- ADD SALT FOR FLAVOR
- ADD ALL TO MOULD AND COVER.
- KEEP IN REF ( NOT FREEZER ) FOR 2 HRS AFTER COOLING DOWN.
