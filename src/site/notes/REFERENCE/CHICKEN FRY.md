## INGREDIENTS
- CHICKEN MEDIUM SIZED
- GINGER GARLIC PASTE
- CHILLI POWDER
- TURMERIC POWDER
- GARAM MASALA
- PEPPER, SALT
- VINEGAR/LEMON SQUEEZE

## PREPARATION 
- MARINATE ALL INGREDIENTS 3 HRS
- ADD 3 TABLE SPOON CORNFLOUR 
- FRY
