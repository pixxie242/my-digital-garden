## Ingredients

- Beef 450g
- onion, garlic, ginger
- tomato
- chilli
- coriander
## Preparation
1. Marinate with tomato, ginger garlic paste, chilli, turmeric, chilli, coriander, salt, yogurt, coriander.
2. Saute onion, garlic until brown.
3. Add marinated meat
4. saute until dry.
